Introduction

A soft voice for a serious ethic.

Green and Gentle connects three forms of care: environmentalism, vegan practice, and anti-natalist ethics. The shared question is simple: how can we place fewer burdens on the Earth, on animals, on other people, and on possible future beings?

This home page stays intentionally simple. The deeper material lives on separate pages so the site can grow without becoming crowded.
